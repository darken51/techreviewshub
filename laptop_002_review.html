Key Features and Specifications

Before we get into the nitty-gritty, let's take a look at what makes the MacBook Air M3 tick:

  • Processor: Apple M3 chip with 8-core CPU and 10-core GPU
  • RAM: 16GB of fast LPDDR5 memory for seamless multitasking

  • Display: 13.6-inch Retina display with P3 color gamut and 500 nits brightness

  • Ports: Two Thunderbolt 4 ports, one USB-A port, and a headphone jack
